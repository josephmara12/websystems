HSC

The HSC was a tough and stressful time. I chose to study maths, English, business, economics and IPT. IPT was where I got a taste of what IT would be like in university learning about networks, databases and programming.

Computing

Throughout school I undertook many projects in high-school including building a Unity game, creating a human vs. computer TIC TAC TOE game and creating a fully functional access database.

Work

In regards to work, I went straight from school to becoming an ICT assistant helping students with their IT issues whilst also setting up all the devices. I am also currently working at Telstra.
